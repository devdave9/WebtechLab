BTech

"The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program at PDEU is a four-year undergraduate degree that focuses on developing innovative solutions for the energy sector. The program offers specializations in disciplines such as Petroleum Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Chemical Engineering. The curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in mathematics, science, and engineering principles. Students also have access to state-of-the-art laboratories, research facilities, and industry partnerships. PDEU's B.Tech program is accredited by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC)."

MTech

"The Master of Technology (M.Tech) program at PDEU is a two-year postgraduate degree that focuses on advanced education and research in energy-related fields. The program offers specializations in disciplines such as Petroleum Engineering, Renewable Energy, Energy Systems, and Power Systems. The curriculum is designed to provide students with in-depth knowledge and expertise in their chosen specialization. Students also have access to state-of-the-art laboratories, research facilities, and industry partnerships. PDEU's M.Tech program is accredited by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC)."

CSE

"The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at PDEU offers a 4-year Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program. The curriculum focuses on developing strong foundational knowledge in computer science and engineering. Students learn programming languages, data structures, algorithms, computer networks, and database management systems. The program also emphasizes hands-on experience through projects, internships, and research opportunities. PDEU's CSE program is designed to prepare students for successful careers in the IT industry, research, and entrepreneurship."

ICT

"The ICT Department at PDEU focuses on education, research, and innovation in information and communication technology. The department offers B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D. programs in ICT. The curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in ICT and its applications. The department has state-of-the-art laboratories and research facilities, and faculty members with expertise in various areas of ICT. The ICT Department at PDEU aims to prepare students for successful careers in the ICT industry and academia."

SOT Library

"The SOT Library at PDEU is a state-of-the-art library that supports the academic and research needs of students and faculty. The library has a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources in the fields of energy, technology, and management. The library is equipped with modern facilities, including Wi-Fi, digital databases, and online resources. SLS Library
